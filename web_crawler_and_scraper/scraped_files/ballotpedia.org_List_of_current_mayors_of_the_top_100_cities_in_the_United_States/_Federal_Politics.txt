<h2><span>Welcome to Ballotpedia's federal politics portal</span></h2>
<div>Welcome to the portal for Ballotpedia's coverage of federal politics! Our federal-level projects cover the <a>executive branch</a>, the <a>legislative branch</a>, the <a>judicial branch</a>, and <a>presidential candidates</a>. Writers on Ballotpedia's federal projects covered the following elections in 2024:
<ul><li><a>The presidential election</a></li>
<li>33 <a>U.S. Senate regular elections</a></li>
<li>435 <a>U.S. House of Representatives regular elections</a></li>
<li><a>Special elections to the 118th United States Congress (2023-2024)</a></li></ul>
Our goal is to make information about what is happening at the federal level, from elections to legislation, accessible to the average citizen. Our coverage keeps our readers current and informed on the decisions being made in the highest offices in the country.</div>
