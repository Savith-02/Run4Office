- **Position Titles and Descriptions:**
  - **Mayors**: Chief executives of cities, with varying powers depending on the type of government.
  - **Types of Government**:
    - **Strong Mayor**: The mayor serves as the chief executive, and the city council is the primary legislative body.
    - **Council-Manager**: An elected city council appoints a city manager to oversee daily operations.
    - **City Commission**: A council composed of an elected mayor and commissioners serves as both legislative and administrative body.
    - **Hybrid**: The mayor serves as chief executive but appoints a city manager for day-to-day operations.
