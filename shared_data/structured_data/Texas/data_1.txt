Role: Mayor

Location: Texas (Cities - Austin, Corpus Christi, El Paso, Lubbock)

- The mayoral elections for Austin, Corpus Christi, El Paso, and Lubbock in Texas are scheduled to take place in 2024.

Role Responsibilities:
- The mayor serves as the chief executive of the city. The extent of their powers varies depending on the type of city government. 
- In a 'Strong Mayor' type of government, the mayor serves as the city's chief executive with significant powers. 
- In a 'Council-Manager' type of government, an elected city council appoints a city manager to oversee day-to-day operations, but the mayor still retains certain executive powers.
- In a 'City Commission' type of government, a city council consisting of elected officials acts as both the legislative and administrative body.
- In a 'Hybrid' type of government, the mayor has executive powers but also appoints a city manager.
